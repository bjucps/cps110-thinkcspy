Summary
-------

-  A sequential search is :math:`O(n)` for ordered and unordered
   lists.

-  A binary search of an ordered list is :math:`O(\log n)` in the
   worst case.

-  A bubble sort, a selection sort, and an insertion sort are
   :math:`O(n^{2})` algorithms.
